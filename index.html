About Me

Hi, my name is Daniel, currently 27 years old... I live in Chiapas, Mexico. I am a student at BYU Idaho, I'm currently enrolled in WDD 131. I love to take some pictures, and I enjoy going outside on trips, sometimes I do some hiking.

I think we talk about México all the time but now I want to talk about my state. Chiapas is an amazing state in the south of Mexico, its near to Guatemala and also it is home of the Deepest Canyon in North America; The "Cañon del Sumidero" has cliffs that rise over 1,000 meters (3,300 feet) from the Grijalva River. This canyon, which is even deeper than the Grand Canyon in some sections, was formed by seismic faulting millions of years ago and is now a stunning national park.
